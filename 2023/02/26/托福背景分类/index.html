1.Sun

The Sun is the Solar System’s star and by far its most massive component. Its large mass (332,900 Earth masses), which comprises 99.86% of all the mass in the Solar System, produces temperatures and densities in its core high enough to sustain nuclear fusion of hydrogen into helium, making it a main sequence star. This releases an enormous amout of energy, mostly radiated into space as electromagnetic radiation peaking in visible light.

太阳是太阳系的恒星，也是迄今为止质量最大的组成部分。 它的大质量（332,900 个地球质量）占太阳系总质量的 99.86%，在其核心产生的温度和密度足以维持氢核聚变为氦，使其成为一颗主序星。 这释放出巨大的能量，大部分以可见光中达到峰值的电磁辐射形式辐射到太空中。

The Sun is a population I star; it has a higher abundance of elements heavier than hydrogen and helium than the older population II stars. Elements heavier than hydrogen and helium were formed in the cores of ancient and exploding stars, so the first generation of stars had to die before the Universe could be enriched with these atoms. The oldest stars contain few metals, whereas born later have more. This high metallicity is thought to have been crucial to the Sun’s development of a planetary system because the planets form from the accretion of “metals”.

太阳是I星群； 与年龄较大的星族 II 恒星相比，它拥有更多比氢和氦重的元素。 比氢和氦更重的元素是在古老的爆炸恒星的核心中形成的，因此第一代恒星必须在宇宙中充满这些原子之前死亡。 最古老的恒星含有很少的金属，而较晚诞生的则含有更多。 这种高金属性被认为对太阳行星系统的发展至关重要，因为行星是由“金属”的增生形成的。

2. Inner Solar System

The inner Solar System is the region comprising the terrestrial planets and the asteroid belt. Composed mainly of silicates and metals, the objects of the inner Solar System are relatively close to the Sun; the radius of this entire region is less than the distance between the orbits of Jupiter and Saturn.

内太阳系是由类地行星和小行星带组成的区域。 内太阳系天体主要由硅酸盐和金属组成，距离太阳较近； 整个区域的半径小于木星和土星轨道之间的距离。

The four terrestrial or inner planets have dense, rocky compositions, few or no moons, and no ring systems. They are composed largely of refractory minerals, such as the silicates—which form their crusts and mantles—and metals, such as iron and nickel, which form their cores. Three of the four inner planets (Venus, Earth and Mars) have atmospheres substantial enough to generate weather; all have impact craters and tectonic surface features, such as rift valleys and volcanoes.

这四颗类地行星或内行星具有致密的岩石成分，很少或没有卫星，也没有环系统。 它们主要由耐火矿物组成，例如形成地壳和地幔的硅酸盐，以及形成地核的铁和镍等金属。 四颗内行星中的三颗（金星、地球和火星）的大气足以产生天气； 所有这些都有撞击坑和构造表面特征，例如裂谷和火山。

Mercury

Mercury (0.4 AU from the Sun) is the closest planet to the Sun and on average, all seven other planets. The smallest planet in the Solar System, Mercury has no natural satellites. Besides impact craters, its only known geological features are lobed ridges or rupes that were probably produced by a period of contraction early in its history. Mercury’s very tenuous atmosphere consists of atoms blasted off its surface by the solar wind. Its relatively large iron core and thin mantle have not yet been adequately explained. Hypotheses include that its outer layers were stripped off by a giant impact, or that it was prevented from fully accreting by the young Sun’s energy.

水星（距离太阳 0.4 个天文单位）是离太阳最近的行星，平均来说，所有其他七颗行星。 水星是太阳系中最小的行星，没有天然卫星。 除了撞击坑外，它唯一已知的地质特征是可能由其历史早期的一段收缩期产生的叶状山脊或卢比。 水星非常稀薄的大气层由被太阳风吹离其表面的原子组成。 它相对较大的铁核和较薄的地幔尚未得到充分解释。 假设包括它的外层被巨大的撞击剥离，或者它被年轻太阳的能量阻止完全吸积。

Venus

Venus (0.7 AU from the Sun) is close in size to Earth and, like Earth, has a thick silicate mantle around an iron core, a substantial atomsphere, and evidence of internal geological activity. It is much drier than Earth, and its atmosphere is ninety times as dense. Venus has no natural satellites. It is the hottest planet, with surface temperatures over 400℃, most likely due to the amount of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere. No definitive evidence of current geological activity has been detected on Venus, but it has no magnetic field that would prevent depletion of its substantial atmosphere, which suggests that its atmosphere is being replenished by volcanic eruptions.

金星（距太阳 0.7 个天文单位）的大小与地球接近，并且与地球一样，在铁核周围有一层厚厚的硅酸盐地幔、一个坚固的大气层和内部地质活动的证据。 它比地球干燥得多，大气层的密度是地球的九十倍。 金星没有天然卫星。 它是最热的行星，表面温度超过 400 摄氏度，这很可能是由于大气中大量的温室气体所致。 没有在金星上发现当前地质活动的确切证据，但它没有磁场可以防止其大量大气层耗尽，这表明它的大气层正在通过火山喷发得到补充。

Earth

Earth (1 AU from the Sun) is the largest and densest of the inner planets, the only one known to have current geological activity, and the only place where life is known to exist. Its liquid hydrosphere is unique among the terrestrial planets, and it is the only planet where plate tectonics has been observed. Earth’s atmosphere is radically different from those of the other planets, having been altered by the presence of life to contain 21% free oxygen. It has one natural satellite, the Moon, the only large satellite of a terrestrial planet in the Solar System.

地球（距太阳 1 个天文单位）是内行星中最大、密度最大的行星，是已知唯一具有当前地质活动的行星，也是已知存在生命的唯一地方。 它的液态水圈在类地行星中是独一无二的，也是唯一观测到板块构造的行星。 地球的大气层与其他行星的大气层截然不同，它因生命的存在而发生改变，含有 21% 的游离氧。 它有一颗天然卫星，月球，是太阳系中唯一一颗类地行星的大型卫星。

Mars

Mars (1.5 AU from the Sun) is smaller than Earth and Venus. It has an atmosphere of mostly carbon dioxide with a surface pressure of 6.1 millibars (roughly 0.6% of that of Earth). Its surface, peppered with vast volcanoes, such as Olympus Mons, and rift valleys, such as Valles Marineris, shows geological activity that may have persisted until as recently as 2 million years ago. Its red colour comes from iron oxide (rust) in its soil. Mars has two tiny natural satellites (Deimos and Phobos) thought to be either captured asteroids, or ejected debris from a massive impact early in Mars’s history.

火星（距太阳 1.5 个天文单位）比地球和金星小。 它的大气层主要是二氧化碳，表面压力为 6.1 毫巴（大约是地球压力的 0.6%）。 它的表面散布着巨大的火山，如奥林匹斯山和裂谷，如水手谷，表明地质活动可能一直持续到最近的 200 万年前。 它的红色来自土壤中的氧化铁（铁锈）。 火星有两颗微小的天然卫星（Deimos 和 Phobos），它们被认为要么是被捕获的小行星，要么是火星早期历史上一次巨大撞击产生的碎片。

Asteroid belt

Asteroids except for the largest, Ceres, are classified as small Solar System bodies and are composed mainly of refractory rocky and metallic minerals, with some ice. They range from a few metres to hundreds of kilometres in size. Asteroids smaller than one meter are usually called meteoroids and micrometeoroids (grain-sized), depending on different, somewhat arbitrary definitions.

除了最大的谷神星外，小行星都被归类为太阳系小天体，主要由难熔岩石和金属矿物组成，还有一些冰。 它们的大小从几米到数百公里不等。 小于一米的小行星通常被称为流星体和微流星体（颗粒大小），这取决于不同的、有些武断的定义。

The asteroid belt occupies the orbit between Mars and Jupiter, between 2.3 and 3.3 AU from the Sun. It is thought to be remnants from the Solar System’s formation that failed to coalesce because of the gravitational interference of Jupiter. The asteroid belt contains tens of thousands, possibly millions, of objects over one kilometre in diameter. Despite this, the total mass of the asteroid belt is unlikely to be more than a thousandth of that of Earth. The asteroid belt is very sparsely populated; spacecraft routinely pass through without incident.

小行星带位于火星和木星之间的轨道上，距离太阳 2.3 到 3.3 个天文单位。 它被认为是太阳系形成的残余物，由于木星的引力干扰而未能合并。 小行星带包含数万个、可能数百万个直径超过一公里的物体。 尽管如此，小行星带的总质量不太可能超过地球的千分之一。 小行星带人烟稀少； 航天器经常平安无事地通过。

3. Outer Solar System

The outer region of the Solar System is home to the giant planets and their large moons. The centaurs and many short-period comets also orbit in this region. Due to their greater distance from the Sun, the solid objects in the outer Solar System contain a higher proportion of volatiles, such as water, ammonia, and methane than those of the inner Solar System because the lower temperatures allow these compounds to remain solid.

太阳系的外部区域是巨行星及其大卫星的家园。 半人马和许多短周期彗星也在这个区域运行。 由于与太阳的距离较远，外太阳系中的固体物体比内太阳系的固体物体含有更高比例的挥发物，例如水、氨和甲烷，因为较低的温度允许这些化合物保持固态。

The four outer planets, or giant planets (sometimes called Jovian planets), collectively make up 99% of the mass known to orbit the Sun. Jupiter and Saturn are together more than 400 times the mass of Earth and consist overwhelmingly of the gases hydrogen and helium, hence their designation as gas giants. Uranus and Neptune are far less massive—less than 20 Earth masses each—and are composed primarily of ices. For these reasons, some astronomers suggest they belong in their own category, ice giants. All four giant planets have rings, although only Saturn’s ring system is easily observed from Earth. The term superior planet designates planets outside Earth’s orbit and thus includes both the outer planets and Mars.

四颗外行星或巨行星（有时称为木星行星）合计占已知绕太阳运行质量的 99%。 木星和土星的质量是地球的 400 多倍，并且绝大多数由氢气和氦气组成，因此它们被称为气态巨行星。 天王星和海王星的质量要小得多——每个都不到地球质量的 20 个——并且主要由冰组成。 由于这些原因，一些天文学家认为它们属于自己的类别，即冰巨星。 所有四颗巨行星都有光环，但从地球上只能轻松观察到土星的光环系统。 高级行星一词指的是地球轨道之外的行星，因此包括外行星和火星。

Jupiter

Jupiter is 2.5 times the mass of all the other planets put together. It is composed largely of hydrogen and helium. Jupiter’s strong internal heat creates semi-permanent features in its atmosphere, such as cloud bands and the Great Red Spot. Jupiter has 79 known satellites. The four largest, Ganymede, Callisto, Io, and Europa, show similarities to the terrestrial planets, such as volcanism and internal heating. Ganymede, the largest satellite in the Solar System, is larger than Mercury.

木星的质量是所有其他行星质量总和的 2.5 倍。 它主要由氢和氦组成。 木星强大的内部热量在其大气层中产生了半永久性特征，例如云带和大红斑。 木星有 79 颗已知卫星。 最大的四颗行星，木卫三、木卫四、木卫一和欧罗巴，显示出与类地行星的相似之处，例如火山活动和内部加热。 木卫三是太阳系中最大的卫星，比水星还大。

Saturn

Saturn, distinguished by its extensive ring system, has several similarities to Jupiter, such as its atmospheric composition and magnetosphere. Although Saturn has 60% of Jupiter’s volume, it is less than a third as massive. Saturn is the only planet of the Solar System that is less dense than water. The rings of Saturn are made up of small ice and rock particles. Saturn has 82 confirmed satellites composed largely of ice. Two of these, Titan and Enceladus, show signs of geological activity. Titan, the second-largest moon in the Solar System, is larger than Mercury and the only satellite in the Solar System with a substantial atmosphere.

土星以其广泛的环系统而著称，与木星有一些相似之处，例如其大气成分和磁层。 尽管土星的体积是木星的 60%，但它的质量还不到木星的三分之一。 土星是太阳系中唯一一颗密度低于水的行星。 土星环由小冰和岩石颗粒组成。 土星有 82 颗已确认的主要由冰组成的卫星。 其中两个，土卫六和土卫二，显示出地质活动的迹象。 土卫六是太阳系中第二大卫星，比水星大，是太阳系中唯一一颗拥有大量大气层的卫星。

Uranus

Uranus, is the lightest of the outer planets. Uniquely among the planets, it orbits the Sun on its side; its axial tilt is over ninety degrees to the ecliptic. It has a much colder core than the other giant planets and radiates very little heat into space. Uranus has 27 known satellites, the largest ones being Titania, Oberon, Umbriel, Ariel, and Miranda.

天王星，是最轻的外行星。 在行星中独一无二的是，它绕着太阳公转； 它的轴向倾斜度超过黄道九十度。 它的核心比其他巨行星冷得多，向太空辐射的热量非常少。 天王星有 27 颗已知卫星，最大的是 Titania、Oberon、Umbriel、Ariel 和 Miranda。

Neptune

Neptune, though slightly smaller than Uranus, is more massive and hence more dense. It radiates more internal heat, but not as much as Jupiter or Saturn. Neptune has 14 known satellites. The largest, Triton, is geologically active, with geysers of liquid nitrogen. Triton is the only large satellite with a retrograde orbit. Neptune is accompanied in its orbit by several minor planets, termed Neptune trojans, that are in 1:1 resonance with it.

海王星虽然比天王星略小，但质量更大，因此密度更大。 它散发出更多的内部热量，但没有木星或土星那么多。 海王星有 14 颗已知卫星。 最大的 Triton 地质活动活跃，有液氮间歇泉。 Triton 是唯一一颗具有逆行轨道的大型卫星。 海王星在其轨道上伴随着几颗小行星，这些小行星被称为海王星特洛伊木马，它们与海王星以 1:1 共振。

工业革命

背景介绍

Overview：

The Industrial Revolution was the transition to new manufacturing processes in Europe and the United States, in the period from about 1760 to sometime between 1820 and 1840. This transition included going from hand production methods to machines, new chemical manufacturing and iron production processes, the increasing use of steam power and water power, the development of machine tools and the rise of the mechanized factory system. The Industrial Revolution also led to an unprecedented rise in the rate of population growth.

工业革命是从大约 1760 年到 1820 年至 1840 年期间欧洲和美国向新制造工艺的过渡。这种过渡包括从手工生产方法到机器、新的化学制造和铁生产工艺， 蒸汽动力和水力的使用增加，机床的发展和机械化工厂系统的兴起。 工业革命还导致人口增长率空前上升。

Textiles were the dominant industry of the Industrial Revolution in terms of employment, value of output and capital invested. The textile industry was also the first to use modern production methods.

就就业、产值和投资资本而言，纺织业是工业革命的主导产业。 纺织业也是最早使用现代生产方法的行业。

The Industrial Revolution marks a major turning point in history; almost every aspect of daily life was influenced in some way. In particular, average income and population began to exhibit unprecedented sustained growth. Some economists have said the most important effect of the Industrial Revolution was that the standard of living for the general population in the western world began to increase consistently for the first time in history, although others have said that it did not begin to meaningfully improve until the late 19th and 20th centuries.

工业革命标志着历史的重大转折点。 几乎日常生活的方方面面都受到了某种程度的影响。 特别是平均收入和人口开始出现前所未有的持续增长。 一些经济学家表示，工业革命最重要的影响是西方世界普通民众的生活水平在历史上首次开始持续提高，尽管其他人表示，工业革命直到 19 世纪末和 20 世纪。

Requirement

Six factors facilitated industrialization:

六大因素促进产业化：

  1. high levels of agricultural productivity to provide excess manpower and food;

  2. a pool of managerial and entrepreneurial skills;

  3. available ports, rivers, canals and roads to cheaply move raw materials and outputs;

  4. natural resources such as coal, iron and waterfalls;

  5. political stability and a legal system that supported business;

  6. financial capital available to invest.

    1. 高水平的农业生产力，以提供多余的人力和食物；

    2. 管理和创业技能库；

    3. 可用于廉价运输原材料和产出的港口、河流、运河和道路；

    4. 煤炭、铁和瀑布等自然资源；

    5. 政治稳定和支持商业的法律制度；

    6. 可用于投资的金融资本。

Once industrialization began in Great Britain, new factors can be added: the eagerness British entrepreneurs to export industrial expertise and the willingness to import the process. Britain met the criteria and industrialized starting in the 18th century. Britain exported the process to western Europe (especially Belgium, France and the German states) in the early 19th century. The United States copied the British model in the early 19th century and Japan copied the Western European models in the late 19th century.

一旦英国开始工业化，就可以添加新的因素：英国企业家出口工业专业知识的渴望和进口该过程的意愿。 英国符合标准并从 18 世纪开始工业化。 19 世纪初，英国将该工艺出口到西欧（尤其是比利时、法国和德国）。 美国在19世纪初复制了英国模式，日本在19世纪末复制了西欧模式。

Important technological development

The commencement of the Industrial Revolution is closely linked to a small number of innovations, beginning in the second half of the 18th century. By the 1830s the following gains had been made in important technologies:

工业革命的开始与从 18 世纪下半叶开始的少数创新密切相关。 到 1830 年代，重要技术取得了以下进展：

1. Textiles – mechanised cotton spinning powered by steam or water increased the output of a worker by a factor of around 500. The power loom increased the output of a worker by a factor of over 40. The cotton gin increased productivity of removing seed from cotton by a factor of 50. Large gains in productivity also occurred in spinning and weaving of wool and linen, but they were not as great as in cotton.

  1. 纺织——以蒸汽或水为动力的机械化棉纺使工人的产量提高了约 500 倍。动力织机使工人的产量提高了 40 多倍。轧棉机提高了从中提取种子的生产率 棉花增长了 50 倍。羊毛和亚麻的纺纱和织造生产率也有很大提高，但不如棉花那么大。

2. Steam power – the efficiency of steam engines increased so that they used between one-fifth and one-tenth as much fuel. The adaptation of stationary steam engines to rotary motion made them suitable for industrial uses. The high pressure engine had a high power to weight ratio, making it suitable for transportation. Steam power underwent a rapid expansion after 1800.

  1. 蒸汽动力——蒸汽机的效率提高了，所以它们使用的燃料只有原来的五分之一到十分之一。 固定式蒸汽机适应旋转运动使其适合工业用途。 高压发动机具有高功率重量比，适合运输。 蒸汽动力在 1800 年后经历了快速扩张。

3. Iron making – the substitution of coke for charcoal greatly lowered the fuel cost of pig iron and wrought iron production. Using coke also allowed larger blast furnaces, resulting in economies of scale. The steam engine began being used to pump water and to power blast air in the mid 1750s, enabling a large increase in iron production by overcoming the limitation of water power. The cast iron blowing cylinder was first used in 1760. It was later improved by making it double acting, which allowed higher blast furnace temperatures. The puddling process produced a structural grade iron at a lower cost than the finery forge. The rolling mill was fifteen times faster than hammering wrought iron. Hot blast (1828) greatly increased fuel efficiency in iron production in the following decades.

  1. 炼铁——焦炭替代木炭大大降低了生铁和熟铁生产的燃料成本。 使用焦炭还允许更大的高炉，从而产生规模经济。 蒸汽机在 1750 年代中期开始用于抽水和为鼓风提供动力，通过克服水力的限制使铁产量大幅增加。 铸铁鼓风缸于 1760 年首次使用。后来改进为双作用，允许更高的高炉温度。 炼钢工艺生产结构级铁的成本低于精细锻造。 轧机比锤打锻铁快十五倍。 在接下来的几十年里，热风技术（1828 年）极大地提高了炼铁生产的燃料效率。

4. Invention of machine tools – The first machine tools were invented. These included the screw cutting lathe, cylinder boring machine and the milling machine. Machine tools made the economical manufacture of precision metal parts possible, although it took several decades to develop effective techniques.

  1. 机床的发明——发明了第一台机床。 其中包括螺纹切削车床、圆筒镗床和铣床。 机床使精密金属零件的经济制造成为可能，尽管开发有效的技术需要几十年的时间。

农业起源

First Agricultural Revolution 也可以称之为Neolithic Revolution

农业起源必定意味着——–人类生活方式的变化，人类健康的变化，社会结构的变化和人口的增多。托福的考试一般不会考这么广，一般只会考其中的一个地点和部分。

Overview：Neolithic Revolution

  • Hunting and gathering to agriculture and settlement

The Neolithic Revolution, or the (First) Agricultural Revolution, was the wide-scale transition of many human cultures during the Neolithic period from a lifestyle of hunting and gathering to one of agriculture and settlement, making an increasingly large population possible. These settled communities permitted humans to observe and experiment with plants to learn how they grew and developed. This new knowledge led to the domestication of plants.

新石器时代革命，或（第一次）农业革命，是新石器时代许多人类文化从狩猎和采集生活方式向农业和定居生活方式的大规模转变，这使得越来越多的人口成为可能。 这些定居的社区允许人类观察和试验植物，以了解它们是如何生长和发育的。 这种新知识导致了植物的驯化。

  • Happaned in separate location worldwide

Archaeological data indicates that the domestication of various types of plants and animals happened in separate locations worldwide, starting in the geological epoch of the Holocene 11,700 years ago. It was the world’s first historically verifiable revolution in agriculture. The Neolithic Revolution greatly narrowed the diversity of foods available, resulting in a downturn in the quality of human nutrition.

考古资料表明，从 11700 年前的全新世地质时代开始，各种动植物的驯化发生在世界各地的不同地点。 这是世界上第一次历史可验证的农业革命。 新石器时代革命极大地缩小了可用食物的多样性，导致人类营养质量下降。

  • Food-crop cultivation

The Neolithic Revolution involved far more than the adoption of a limited set of food-producing techniques. During the next millennia it transformed the small and mobile groups of hunter-gatherers that had hitherto dominated human pre-history into sedentary (non-nomadic) societies based in built-up villages and towns. These societies radically modified their natural environment by means of specialized food-crop cultivation, with activities such as irrigation and deforestation which allowed the production of surplus food.

新石器时代革命涉及的远不止采用一套有限的食品生产技术。 在接下来的几千年里，它把迄今为止主导人类史前时期的小型、流动的狩猎采集者群体转变为以建成的村庄和城镇为基础的定居（非游牧）社会。 这些社会通过专门的粮食作物种植彻底改变了他们的自然环境，包括灌溉和砍伐森林等活动，从而生产出剩余的食物。

  • Domestication of animals

Other developments that are found very widely during this era are the deomestication of animals, pottery, polished stone tools, and rectangular houses. In many regions, the adoption of agriculture by prehistoric societies caused episodes of rapid population growth, a phenomenon known as the Neolithic demographic transition.

在这个时代广泛发现的其他发展是动物、陶器、抛光石器和矩形房屋的驯化。 在许多地区，史前社会对农业的采用导致了人口的快速增长，这种现象被称为新石器时代的人口转变。

Two important lifestyle

  1. Nomadism lifestyle

Hunter-gatherers had different subsistence requirements and lifestyles from agriculturalists. They resided in temporary shelters and were highly mobile, moving in small groups and had limited contact with outsiders. Their diet was well-balanced and depended on what the environment provided each season.

狩猎采集者与农业者有着不同的生存要求和生活方式。 他们住在临时避难所，流动性强，成群结队，与外界接触有限。 他们的饮食很均衡，取决于每个季节所提供的环境。

  1. Settlement lifestyle

Because the advent of agriculture made it possible to support larger groups, agriculturalists lived in more permanent dwellings in areas that were more densely populated than could be supported by the hunter-gatherer lifestyle. The development of trading networks and complex societies brought them into contact with outside groups.

由于农业的出现使养活更大的群体成为可能，因此农学家住在人口稠密地区的永久住所中，而狩猎采集生活方式无法支持这些地区。 贸易网络和复杂社会的发展使他们与外部团体接触。

几个重要的问题：

  • Why agriculturalists are not health?

However, population increase did not necessarily correlate with improved health. Reliance on a single crop can adversely affect health even while making it possible to support larger numbers of people. Maize is deficient in certain essential amino acids (lysine and tryptophan) and is a poor source of iron. The phytic acid it contains may inhibit nutrient absorption. Other factors that likely affected the health of early agriculturalists and their domesticated livestock would have been increased numbers of parasites and disease-bearing pests associated with human waste and contaminated food and water supplies. Fertilizers and irrigation may have increased crop yields but also would have promoted proliferation of insects and bacteria in the local environment while grain storage attracted additional insects and rodents.

然而，人口增加并不一定与健康状况改善相关。 对单一作物的依赖可能会对健康产生不利影响，即使这样可以养活更多的人。 玉米缺乏某些必需氨基酸（赖氨酸和色氨酸），并且是铁的不良来源。 它所含的植酸可能会抑制营养吸收。 其他可能影响早期农学家及其家养牲畜健康的因素包括与人类排泄物以及受污染的食物和水供应相关的寄生虫和携带疾病的害虫数量增加。 施肥和灌溉可能会增加作物产量，但也会促进当地环境中昆虫和细菌的繁殖，而粮食储存会吸引更多的昆虫和啮齿动物。

  • What caused the Agricultural revolution?

The Oasis Theory

The Oasis Theory originally proposed by Raphael Pumpelly in 1908, popularized by V. Gordon Childe in 1928 and summarised in Childe’s book Man Makes Himself. This theory maintains that as the climate got drier due to the Atlantic depressions shifting northward, communities contracted to oases where they were forced into close association with animals, which were then domesticated together with planting of seeds. However, today this theory has little support amongst archaeologists because subsequent climate data suggests that the region was getting wetter rather than drier.

绿洲理论最初由 Raphael Pumpelly 于 1908 年提出，1928 年由 V. Gordon Childe 推广并总结于 Childe 的著作 Man Makes Himself。 该理论认为，由于大西洋洼地向北移动导致气候变得干燥，社区承包到绿洲，在那里他们被迫与动物密切联系，然后在种植种子的同时被驯化。 然而，今天这一理论在考古学家中几乎没有得到支持，因为随后的气候数据表明该地区变得更湿润而不是更干燥。

The Stable Climate Theory

Peter Richerson, Robert Boyd, and Robert Bettinger make a case for the development of agriculture coinciding with an increasingly stable climate at the beginning of the Holocene. Ronald Wright’s book and Massey Lecture Series A Short History of Progress popularized this hypothesis.

彼得·里森 (Peter Richerson)、罗伯特·博伊德 (Robert Boyd) 和罗伯特·贝廷格 (Robert Bettinger) 提出了农业发展与全新世初期日益稳定的气候相吻合的案例。 罗纳德·赖特 (Ronald Wright) 的书和梅西系列讲座“进步简史”(A Short History of Progress) 推广了这一假设。

Megafauna extinction Theory

The postulated Younger Dryas impact event, claimed to be in part responsible for megafauna extinction and ending the last glacial period, could have provided circumstances that required the evolution of agricultural societies for humanity to survive.

假设的新仙女木撞击事件声称对巨型动物的灭绝和末次冰期的结束负有部分责任，可能提供了人类生存所需的农业社会进化的环境。

The Demographic Theories

The Demographic theories proposed by Carl Sauer and adapted by Lewis Binford and Kent Flannery posit an increasingly sedentary population that expanded up to the carrying capacity of the local environment and required more food than could be gathered. Various social and economic factors helped drive the need for food.

卡尔绍尔提出并由刘易斯宾福德和肯特弗兰纳里改编的人口学理论假设越来越多的久坐不动的人口扩大到当地环境的承载能力并且需要比收集更多的食物。 各种社会和经济因素帮助推动了对食物的需求。
